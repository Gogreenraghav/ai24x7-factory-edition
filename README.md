# AI24x7 Factory Edition 🏭

**Ready-Made Industrial AI Surveillance System**

AI-powered 24/7 factory monitoring with PPE compliance, fire detection, equipment monitoring, and automatic shift reports.

## Features

### Safety
- 🔥 Fire & Smoke Detection - 2-3 second alert
- 🪖 PPE Compliance - Helmet, vest, gloves, shoes check
- 🏃 Fall Detection - Worker safety monitoring
- 🛢️ Spill & Hazard Detection - Oil/chemical spills
- ⛔ Danger Zone Monitoring - Restricted area alerts

### Operations
- 🚗 ANPR - Vehicle entry/exit tracking
- ⚙️ Equipment Monitoring - Machine status (running/stopped)
- 📦 Production Counting - Real-time item count
- 🔄 Shift Management - 3-shift system with reports

### Alerts
- 📱 SMS (Damini/Fast2SMS)
- 💬 WhatsApp Business
- ✈️ Telegram
- 🔊 Voice Announcements (Hindi/English TTS)
